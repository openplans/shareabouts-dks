How do you get around Columbia County?

The first step in the development of the Transportation System Plan is assessing what we have. Tell us what you think about the Transportation System in Columbia County by adding comments to the map.

The map provides a place to tell us what you think – and read what others have said – about different transportation issues around the county. Please help us identify what you like about getting around Columbia County as well as opportunities for improvement. Consider questions such as:

  • What do you like about traveling in Columbia County?
  • Is there a particular area that is congested or where you feel unsafe when traveling?
  • How is the transit service in the County?
