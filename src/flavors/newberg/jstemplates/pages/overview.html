Tell us what you think about transportation in Newberg County!

The first step in the development of the Transportation System Plan is assessing what we have. Tell us what you think about the Transportation System in Newberg County by adding comments to the map.

As you make your comments, please consider the following:

  • What do you like about traveling in Newberg?
  • What could be done to make traveling in Dundee better?
  • Is there a particular intersection that you feel is congested?
  • Do you feel unsafe driving, biking or walking at locations around the City?
  • How is the transit service in the City?
