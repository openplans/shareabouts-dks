Tell us what you think about the Transportation System in Cottage Grove by adding comments to the map. Your input matters!

This map provides a place to tell us what you think about transportation issues around the city. Please help us identify what you like about getting around Cottage Grove as well as opportunities for improvement. Think about driving, biking, walking, taking the bus, etc. Consider questions such as:

  • What do you like about traveling in Cottage Grove that should be maintained?
  • Do you feel unsafe driving, biking or walking at locations around the City?
  • Are there places that are difficult to get to?
  • How can transit service in the City be best improved?
  • Is there a particular intersection that you feel is congested?
  • What can be done to make traveling in Cottage Grove better?
