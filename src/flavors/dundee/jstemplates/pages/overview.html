Tell us what you think about transportation in Dundee!

The first step in the development of the Transportation System Plan is assessing what we have. Tell us what you think about the Transportation System in Dundee by adding comments to the map.

As you make your comments, please consider the following:

  • What do you like about traveling in Dundee?
  • What could be done to make traveling in Dundee better?
  • Is there a particular intersection that you feel is congested?
  • Do you feel unsafe driving, biking or walking at locations around the City?
  • How is the transit service in the City?

Read more about the Transportation System Plan at dundeetsp.org.
