Tell us what you think about transportation in Lane County!

The first step in the development of the Transportation System Plan is assessing what we have. Tell us what you think about the transportation system in Lane County by adding comments to the map.

This map provides a place to tell us what you think—and read what others have said—about different transportation issues around the county. Please help us identify what you like about getting around Lane County as well as opportunities for improvement. Consider questions such as:

  • What do you like about traveling in Lane County?
  • Is there a particular roadway or intersection that you feel is unsafe and/or congested?
  • Do you feel unsafe driving, biking, or walking at locations around the County?
  • How is the transit service in the County?
