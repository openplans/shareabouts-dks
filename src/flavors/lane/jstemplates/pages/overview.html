Tell us what you think about transportation in Lane County!

Use this map to tell us what you think—and to read what others have said—about different transportation issues around the County. Consider questions such as:

  • Is there a particular roadway or intersection you feel is congested or is in need of improvement?
  • Do you feel unsafe driving, biking, walking, or catching the bus at specific locations around the County?
  • What transportation improvements would you like to see included in the updated Transportation System Plan?
  • What connections or other improvements are needed for transit service in the County?

Explore issues identified by others, and add your comments and clicks of support.
